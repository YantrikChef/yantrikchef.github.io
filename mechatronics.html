Mechatronics Engineer

Job Description

We are seeking a self-motivated, focused, highly skilled and experienced Mechatronics Engineer to join our food-tech startup and lead the design and development of special purpose machines. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in CAD, product design & development, and hands-on experience in integrating mechanical, electrical, and control systems. Knowledge or interest in automation will be considered an added advantage.

Responsibilities

  1. Design and Development
    • Utilize CAD software (e.g., SolidWorks, AutoCAD) to conceptualize, design, and develop special purpose machines.
    • Create detailed engineering drawings, schematics, and layouts for mechanical and electrical systems.
    • Conduct feasibility studies, simulations, and engineering calculations to validate design concepts.
  2. Mechanical Integration
    • Select and integrate motors, gears, actuators, and other mechanical components suitable for the machine’s functionality.
    • Design mechanical systems that meet performance, reliability, and safety requirements.
  3. Electrical and Electronic Systems
    • Design and implement electrical and electronic systems, including power supplies, voltage regulation circuits, microcontrollers, and PCBs.
    • Ensure compatibility and seamless integration of electrical components with mechanical systems.
  4. Prototyping and Testing
    • Build prototypes and conduct testing to verify design performance and functionality.
    • Identify and troubleshoot technical issues during prototype development and testing phases.
  5. Automation and Control Systems
    • Apply knowledge of automation principles to enhance machine functionality and operational efficiency.
    • Program microcontrollers and develop control algorithms for automated processes.
  6. Project Management
    • Manage projects from initial concept through design, development, testing, and deployment
    • Coordinate with cross-functional teams, including mechanical engineers, electrical engineers, software developers, and manufacturing personnel.
  7. Documentation and Reporting
    • Maintain comprehensive documentation of design specifications, test results, and technical reports.
    • Prepare user manuals, assembly instructions, and technical documentation for end-users and internal stakeholders.

Qualifications & Skills

  1. Education
    • Master’s/Bachelor’s degree in Mechatronics (M.Tech. /M.E. /B.Tech. /B.E.) or a related field. Advanced degrees are a plus.
  2. Experience
    • Minimum of 4+ years of experience in mechatronics design and development, specifically in designing special purpose machines.
    • Proven track record of successfully leading projects from concept to production, including hands-on prototyping and testing.
  3. Technical Skills
    • Proficiency in CAD software (SolidWorks, AutoCAD) for mechanical design and simulation.
    • Strong knowledge of product design & development processes.
    • Experience with motors, gears, actuators, and other mechanical components.
    • Design and implementation of power supplies, voltage regulation circuits, and PCBs.
    • Programming and integration of microcontrollers for control systems.
    • Familiarity with automation principles and technologies.
  4. Other Skills
    • Excellent problem-solving abilities and analytical skills.
    • Strong project management capabilities, including budget and timeline management.
    • Effective communication skills and ability to collaborate within a multidisciplinary team environment.
    • Attention to detail and commitment to delivering high-quality solutions.
  5. Preferred Qualities
    • Experience in industrial automation or robotics.
    • Knowledge of PLC programming and control systems.
    • Familiarity with regulatory standards and compliance requirements in relevant industries.

Work Environment

    • This position is based in [Kolkata], no travelling require.
    • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced and dynamic work environment.
    • This position involves working in a manufacturing plant, production floor, or workshop environment.
    • Ability to work flexible hours and occasional overtime as needed
