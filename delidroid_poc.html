DeliDroid Ver 0.1 - A Proof of Concept

At YantrikChef, we are at the forefront of food automation technology, combining robotics, AI, and IoT to revolutionize food preparation and delivery. This Proof of Concept (POC) video demonstrates the capabilities of our DeliDroid unit, a robotic culinary kiosk designed to prepare and serve freshly made gourmet sandwiches on-demand.

Features Demonstrated in this POC

  • Bread Dispenser: Automatically dispenses bread slices
  • Butter Applicator: Applies butter to the bread slices
  • Toppings Dispenser: Adds selected toppings to the sandwich
  • Sauce Dispenser: Dispenses sauces with precision
  • Condiments Sprinkler: Sprinkles condiments as per the order

Our prototype is constructed using mild-steel angles, water pipes, PVC sheets, and aluminum panels and rods, all meticulously joined and glued together. The system is controlled using microcontrollers like Arduino UNO and NANO, equipped with various sensors, relays, and drivers to ensure precise operation.

 

The video showcases an order being placed from a laptop and sent to the Principal Controller Arduino UNO via USB connection. Each section of the DeliDroid then operates through its specific controller to create a sandwich from scratch, demonstrating the seamless integration of hardware and software in our solution.

While this POC demonstrates the basic functionalities, the actual DeliDroid will feature a lot more advanced capabilities, offering greater customization and efficiency. This is a basic design aimed at testing the feasibility of the project.

This POC marks a significant milestone in our journey to develop the first prototype of the DeliDroid. We believe this innovative technology has the potential to redefine how food is prepared and delivered, bringing unparalleled convenience and quality to consumers.
