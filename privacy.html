Privacy Policy

At YantrikChef Pvt. Ltd, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide when using our autonomous food machines or visiting our website.

  1. Information Collection and Use:
    We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details (email address, phone number), and payment information when you place an order through our autonomous food machines or online ordering system.
    We use this information to process your orders, provide customer support, and personalize your experience with our services.
  2. Data Sharing and Disclosure:
    We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business and delivering our services (e.g., payment processors, delivery partners).
    We may also disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests from authorities.
  3. Data Security:
    We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. This includes encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.
  4. Data Retention:
    We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by applicable laws and regulations.
  5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:
    Our website and autonomous food machines may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather analytics data.
    You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  6. Children's Privacy:
    Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
  7. User Rights and Choices:
    You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal information, as well as the right to object to or restrict certain data processing activities.
    To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us using the information provided below.
  8. Updates and Changes:
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically.
